Kimberly Reddick


I grew up in a small town named Weleetka, Oklahoma. My high school graduating class had around 60 people.

After High School, I started at Oklahoma University as a Mechanical Engineer. My family wanted an engineer. I transferred to Oklahoma State University and finally realized that Engineering just wasn't for me. I switched to Computer Science and have been loving it ever since.

I wanted to be a Software Programmer. My Unix knowledge drove me towards a path of Software Support. I feel lucky to have had the opportunity to learn a lot of different technologies and software tools. I found Configuration Management to resonate within me. I love processes and automation.

I have created a mobile hangman-style game for Apple and Google Play stores using Unity. It isn't available in the stores now. I do have a Roblox game available called "Escape Dracula's Castle." What surprised me the most was how much I enjoyed the graphic design portion of game design.

Digital Art classes have been keeping me busy in my free time. I am learning to use Procreate on iPad. I have also started learning Clip Studio Paint EX.

In my professional career, I am working on DevOps Bootcamp. I finally got a class on git and GitHub. Never stop learning is my motto.
